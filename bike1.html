JAWA 353

Topic Content
The Jawa 353 has a number of notable features
Design The Jawa 353 has a symmetrical design and is known for its ease of maintenance and reliability.
Engine The Jawa 353 has a compact engine and rear wheel suspension.
History The Jawa 353 was first developed during World War II and was exported around the world.
Variants The Jawa 353 Type 353 was produced from 1954 to 1962
Technical specifications The Jawa 353 Type 559 has a rectangular tube frame, a bore of 65 mm, a stroke of 75 mm, and a compression ratio of 7.7:1.
Production The Jawa 353 was produced in Czechoslovakia from 1934 to 1972 and in the Czech Republic from 1994 to 2013.
